About

This is the test enviroment for patient summary and e-Prescription chatbot! The built-in chatbot is a proposal update for an already developed application that provides patient summary and e-prescribing information quick and easy.

Patient Summary and e-Prescription are medical documents that include personal medical data, and are part of a person's medical history. They are primarily used in the event of an unexpected or unplanned medical condition to enable healthcare professionals access important medical information.

In order to test the chatbot before add it to the application, a test database was created to hold test users informations. If you want to try the chatbot you can use a test patient id to log-in and see what is inside its personal patient summary and e-Prescription.
